Joanna Brodniak Szymusiak

choreographer / educator / dancer

I am interested in what moves us and organically supports our motivation to move. Dance gives me room to explore without the need to find answers. My movement practice draws from physical training, improvisation, and animalistic inspirations.

Precision of choreography is what i enjoy — the detail, intentionality and multilayered movement give me a sense of order and beauty. In my educational work, I create shared space for sensing the body.

I am a dancer, performer, choreographer and movement educator living in Poland.

Graduated in cultural studies and cultural journalism at the University of Silesia in Katowice (2010-2015), and also completed a certified course for contemporary dance teachers (NCK, Kraków 2010-2012).

I also work as a personal trainer and deep tissue massage therapist - when I'm not dancing, I try to share my knowledge about how to care for the body so it serves us as long and as fruitfully as possible.

Services.

Choreography for performances and projects

Comprehensive creative and movement work for theatres, cultural institutions and independent productions: developing movement language, collaborating with directors, music and lighting teams, and preparing performers.

Dance performances / performance

Solo and group performances in theatrical and outdoor projects, combining precise choreography with improvisation and a somatic approach.

Workshops and movement education

Classes in contemporary dance, improvisation and body awareness for groups and institutions. Creating a safe, mindful space for exploring movement.

Movement direction (theatre, video)

Preparing movement for actors and dancers, staging movement scenes, body coaching, directing support in theatre and music videos.

Solo and duo creations

Creating authorial miniatures (solo/duet) tailored to events, festivals and non-theatrical spaces; from concept to premiere.

Deep tissue massage and body training

Individual work with the body: somatic techniques, mobility, injury prevention. Elements of personal training and working with tissues. Various methods of deep tissue massage.
